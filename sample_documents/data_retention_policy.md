# Data Retention Policy

Application logs are retained for 90 days. Security audit logs are retained for 365 days because they support incident investigation, compliance reporting, and abuse detection. Customer-uploaded documents are retained until the customer deletes them or closes the account.

Deleted customer documents enter a 30 day recovery window. During that period the document can be restored by an account administrator. After the recovery window expires, the document is permanently removed from active storage.

Backups are retained for 35 days and are encrypted at rest. Backup restoration is limited to disaster recovery and cannot be used to restore individual documents after the recovery window has expired.

Customers may request account deletion through the privacy portal. Account deletion removes active user profiles, uploaded documents, and workspace metadata after identity verification is completed. Billing records are retained for 7 years to satisfy tax and accounting obligations.

Employees may access customer content only for support, security, or legal reasons and must record the reason in the access log.
