# Enterprise Service Level Agreement

Enterprise customers receive a monthly uptime commitment of 99.9 percent for the core application. Scheduled maintenance announced at least 72 hours in advance does not count against monthly uptime.

Severity 1 incidents must receive an initial response within 15 minutes for enterprise customers. Severity 2 incidents must receive an initial response within 2 business hours. Severity 3 incidents must receive an initial response within 1 business day.

If monthly uptime falls below 99.9 percent, eligible enterprise customers may request a service credit. The request must be submitted within 30 days after the end of the affected month and must include the account ID, outage dates, and observed impact.

The maximum service credit is 10 percent of the monthly subscription fee for the affected service. Service credits are the customer's sole remedy for missed uptime commitments and cannot be exchanged for cash.

The SLA does not apply to beta features, customer network issues, misuse of the service, or third-party systems outside the provider's control.
