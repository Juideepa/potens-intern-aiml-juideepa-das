# Billing And Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 14 calendar days of the original purchase. Refunds outside the 14 day window are denied unless required by law or approved by finance leadership for a documented service failure.

Monthly subscriptions renew automatically on the renewal date listed in the account dashboard. Cancellation stops the next renewal but does not automatically refund the current billing period. Annual subscriptions may be downgraded at renewal only.

If a customer experienced a confirmed Severity 1 outage for more than four continuous hours, finance may grant a service credit equal to one month of subscription fees. Service credits are applied to the next invoice and are not paid out as cash.

Refund approvals require the invoice ID, account owner email, purchase date, reason for the request, and evidence of duplicate charge or service failure. Support agents may open the request, but only finance can approve it.

Chargebacks immediately suspend refund processing until the payment provider completes its investigation.
